Game-changing hardware to accelerate learning

When: Wednesday, 14 June 2023, 12:30 – 1:30 pm (GMT+10:00)

Microsoft invites you to this virtual webinar to learn how Surface devices transform the teaching and learning experience and empower students and educators in the new era of digital learning.

Students and educators need flexible and versatile devices for all their learning and teaching needs. Surface devices are built with the latest hardware innovations and AI technologies to help you invest in the right technologies for your school.

Discover how Microsoft’s cloud-connected Surface devices power advanced education systems around the world, and deliver secure, accessible, equitable platforms that support school leaders, IT, teachers, and students.

Join this webinar to learn about:

  • The device experience as the education experience. Since COVID, schools and institutions have adapted to remote and hybrid learning by giving students and educators devices. Discover why the type of device is critical to improve teaching and learning in today’s classrooms.
  • The latest transformative teaching and learning solutions. Microsoft’s hardware innovations paired with new, advanced AI technologies can help you invest in the right tools and resources for your school.
  • Microsoft’s cloud-enabled devices. Power your institution with secure, accessible, and equitable platforms to improve all educational devices for your school leaders, IT, teachers, and students.
  • Advanced flexibility for all learning needs. Surface devices are thoughtfully designed with students at the center. They are adaptable for every learning style, helping create engaging learning opportunities in and out of your institution’s classroom.

Microsoft Surface devices are designed to give students and educators more freedom to learn and teach their way from anywhere. Learn how Surface devices are made for education.
